Home

The Xbox Controller is a widely used handheld device for console and PC gaming. Available in both wired and wireless versions, it features nine buttons, two joysticks, and three input ports, all arranged across the front and back, though the majority of the buttons are on the front. The wide variety of colors and accessibility features makes it suitable for a broad range. The website will provide you with the guides to set you up for success when using the controller. It will allow you to configure the controller for Xbox or PC gaming. For easy access to different sections, the links to the sections are provided at the top of the page.

FAQs

  • How do I connect my contoller?: Press the pair button on your console and hold down the pair button on your controller.
  • What batteries does it take?: Two AA batteries.
  • How do I update my controller?: Connected to your console, head to the "Xbox Accessories" app and install updates.
  • Can I use it on other devices?: Yes, it also works on PCs and some smart phones.
  • Can I connect my headset?: Yes, you can plug it into the 3.5mm jack on the bottom of the controller

Troubleshooting

  • Controller won't turn on: First, check if the batteries are correct or charged. If batteries aren't the problem, try to connect the controller using a cable. If that still doesn't work, try resetting your controller by holding the Xbox button for 10 seconds.
  • Buttons aren't working: Restart your console and check for any dirt around the buttons. Use a soft cloth or a cotton swab to clean around the buttons. If issues still occur, you may need to replace the controller.
